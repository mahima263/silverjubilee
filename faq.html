Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need to book hotels?

No. Accommodation will be arranged by IIT Kanpur. Visitor’s Hostel (VH) has already been reserved for the reunion.

Can I bring my spouse and children?

Yes! It’s a family-friendly event and you are encouraged to bring them.

When should I arrive and depart?

Arrive on Dec 26 (preferably by afternoon). Departure suggested on Dec 29 after lunch.

How much does it cost to attend?

There is no charge to attend. Stay and meals are provided. Only travel & incidental expenses are on actual basis.

Will food be provided?

Yes. All meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) will be arranged by IITK.

Is IITK arranging my travel?

Travel to/from Kanpur is your responsibility. Local pickups and group transfers may be arranged.

What’s planned during reunion?

Fun, nostalgia, and meaningful sessions. Detailed agenda will be shared later.

What is expected from me?

Book travel early, share itinerary, and notify if traveling from abroad.

Dress code for reunion?

Warm clothes — Kanpur is cold in December. For photo with Director, wear official reunion T-shirt/hoodie.

How do I confirm participation?

Through a registration link shared via email/WhatsApp. Fill it out early.

Will there be a Yearbook?

Yes. Info and photo submission link will be shared for that.

Will there be a transport survey?

Yes. Shared closer to the event to assist local logistics planning.

Will there be pick-up/drop facilities?

Yes, for major arrival points (railway station, Lucknow airport). Details to follow.

Is accommodation only for alumni?

No, families are welcome. Rooms will be arranged accordingly.

Can I arrange my own stay?

Yes, but it’s recommended to stay at VH to enjoy full reunion experience.

Can I extend my stay?

Yes. Inform the team in advance to facilitate your request.

Will there be campus tours?

Yes, guided campus walks and nostalgia rides will be organized.

Are there activities for kids?

Yes, kids will have dedicated sessions, games, and fun spaces.

Will there be entertainment?

Cultural shows, open mics, informal performances — yes! Volunteer if interested.

Will there be sports?

Yes, friendly games and sports activities are part of the plan.

What is batch fundraising?

It’s a collective effort by alumni to support IITK during milestone reunions.

Why raise funds now?

Because it’s a milestone year — the right time to give back and make an impact.

How are funds used?

For scholarships, research, infrastructure, or any batch-approved initiative.

Can I pledge now, pay later?

Yes. Flexible 3-year fulfillment is allowed to suit your convenience.

Are donations tax exempt?

Yes — 80G (India) and local benefits in US/UK/Canada as applicable.

How to donate or pledge?

Click the “Pledge/Give Back” button and follow the secure process.

Can I donate in installments?

Yes. Monthly/Quarterly/Yearly — all supported with reminders.

Will my name be recognized?

Yes, unless you choose to stay anonymous. Recognition is given on donor walls etc.

Can I donate from overseas?

Yes. Through IITK's global partners. Tax benefits apply as per local laws.

In memory/honor donation possible?

Yes. Email dora_desk@iitk.ac.in to dedicate your contribution.

Who to contact for help?

Email priyadarshini@iitkdf.org or your batch coordinator.

Can I change my pledge?

Yes, contact the alumni team to reschedule or modify.

What if we exceed the target?

Extra funds go to complementary initiatives or scale-up of goal.

When do I get tax receipt?

Within 4–5 working days via email. PAN is mandatory for Indian 80G receipt.

What is batch fundraising?

It’s a collective effort by alumni of a particular batch to raise funds supporting IIT Kanpur. It usually aligns with major reunions.

Why is our batch raising funds now?

It’s our milestone reunion—a time to reflect, reconnect, and give back to support scholarships, research, infrastructure, and more.

How are the funds used?

Funds may support scholarships, faculty chairs, entrepreneurship, or any batch-agreed goal. IITK uses funds per your batch plan.

Can I pledge now and donate later?

Yes! You can pledge now and donate over 3 years. This gives flexibility and helps IITK plan better.

Is my donation tax-exempt?

Yes. 80G benefits apply in India (old regime). US/UK/Canada donors get country-specific benefits. Add PAN for valid Indian receipt.

How do I donate or pledge?

Click the Pledge/Give Back button and fill the secure form with your amount and payment timeline.

Can I donate in installments?

Yes, monthly, quarterly, or annual installments are allowed. You’ll receive reminders accordingly.

How will I know my impact?

Regular updates on campaign progress, stories, photos, and participation data are shared via email and WhatsApp.
