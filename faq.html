Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to attend the reunion?

B.Tech and Dual Degree students who began in 1982, M.Tech and MSc (2-Year) students with roll numbers starting from 84, and PhD students who completed in 1986 are eligible.

How is the registration fee determined?

The fee depends on the number of attendees—more attendees mean lower cost. It’s finalized in consultation with the batch coordinator.

What are the modes of registration fee payment?

Credit card, debit card, Google Pay, and PayPal are accepted.

Is the registration fee refundable in case of cancellation?

No, but it can be transferred to another alumnus upon request.

How can the registration fee be transferred to another alumnus?

Inform your batch coordinator or the designated contact person from IIT Kanpur.

Who is the batch coordinator?

Ajit Das – ajitdass2020@gmail.com

Is accommodation provided for alumni and their families?

Yes, rooms will be arranged at the Visitors’ Hostel and its facilities. Families get individual rooms. Allocation is managed by batch coordinators and Alumni Relations team.

Can I select a room in advance?

No, rooms will not be pre-allocated.

Is lodging arranged for car drivers?

Yes, in the VH dormitory on a shared basis. Meals may be available at hostel messes if requested in advance.

Is driver accommodation included in the registration fee?

No, it needs to be paid separately by the guest.

Are medical facilities available on campus?

Yes, at the Institute Health Centre. Emergency ambulance support is available if required.

Whom should I contact upon arrival at IITK?

Report to your allotted Visitor’s Hostel. Contact your batch coordinator or Ms. Fizzah Zulfikar (Alumni Relations Manager).

Who are the points of contact from IIT Kanpur?

Sakshi Gulati: +91 9936357737
Piyush Saini: +91 8840898982
Fizzah Zulfikar: +91 8004827020

Are transport arrangements available from Kanpur Station?

Yes, buses will be available from the railway station to the IITK campus at specific time slots. Details will be shared before the event.

Are transport arrangements available from Lucknow Airport?

Yes, based on requests. Please notify your arrival time in advance to arrange transportation from Lucknow Airport.

How many people can be accommodated in a room?

Each room typically accommodates 2 adults. Extra mattresses can be arranged on prior request for kids or elders.

Who should I contact for housekeeping requests?

For VH1, call 8171. For International Hostel (VH2), call 4990. Please ensure you're in the room while requesting.

Will bedding be provided in rooms?

Yes, bedding is provided in all rooms at the Visitor’s Hostel and International Hostel.

Will towels and toiletries be provided?

Towels will be provided. Basic toiletries like soap may be included, but it’s recommended to carry personal items.

Will hot water be available?

Yes, hot water is available in all rooms throughout the reunion period.

Can breakfast or lunch be arranged in the halls?

Yes, it can be arranged with prior notice. Coordinate with the alumni office or your batch coordinator for group bookings.

Are campus transport options available?

Yes, e-rickshaws and cabs are available on campus. Trips outside campus are chargeable.

Where can I buy essentials?

The campus shopping centre has general stores, a pharmacy, eateries, and stationery shops.

What is the checkout process?

Return your room key at the VH reception at the time of departure. Additional info will be shared in your welcome kit.

Where will accommodation be provided?

In Visitor’s Hostel (VH1) and International Hostel (VH2). Room numbers will be allotted at check-in.

What type of accommodation is available for families?

Double-bed rooms with options to add extra bedding. Inform in advance if needed for children or elders.

Will heating arrangements be available in rooms during winter?

Yes, all VH rooms are equipped with heat blowers or room heaters.

Can musical instruments be arranged?

Yes, musical instruments can be arranged based on availability. Please inform the alumni team in advance so arrangements can be made accordingly.

Can accommodation be arranged for external performers?

Yes, with prior intimation. Alumni inviting external artists are requested to sponsor their lodging at IITK campus guest houses.

Where are ATMs located on campus?

ATMs of SBI, ICICI, and Canara Bank are available near the shopping center and New SAC area on campus.

How can I find contact details of professors?

You may contact the Alumni Relations team who can coordinate faculty meetings upon request. Emailing your interest in advance is recommended.

What places should I explore on campus?

Don't miss the Library Lane, your department buildings (CSE, EE, ME), old halls of residence, and the newly developed Academic Blocks and New SAC.

Can I meet the Director or senior officials?

Yes, meetings are arranged depending on availability. The Alumni Relations team can help coordinate such interactions.

Can I explore Kanpur city during the reunion?

Yes, you are free to explore. Suggested spots: Z Square Mall, JK Temple, Ganga Barrage, Phool Bagh. Local transport can be arranged on request.

I want to celebrate a special occasion during the reunion. How can I plan it?

Please get in touch with the alumni team. We can assist in arranging cakes, AV displays, and small gatherings based on your needs.

Whom should I contact for groceries or medicines?

The campus shopping center has all essentials including a pharmacy. For urgent needs, you may also ask the front desk for assistance.

What recreational activities are available for families?

Indoor games, movie screenings, kids’ play zones, and informal bonding sessions are planned. Details will be shared in the reunion schedule.

How long is one glider ride and what are the timings?

Each ride lasts around 3–5 minutes. Timings are typically between 7 AM – 10 AM. Final schedule will be shared closer to the date.

Are glider rides first-come, first-served?

Yes, availability is limited. Registration for glider rides will open on Day 1 at the help desk. Early registration is recommended.

Need Any Assistance?
